Jaadu Web Co-Pilot

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026

🔒 The short version: Jaadu does not store, sell, or share your data. Page content you submit is sent to an AI model to generate a response and is immediately discarded — nothing is saved on our servers.

1. What Jaadu Is

Jaadu is a Chrome browser extension that uses AI to help you summarize, explain, and ask questions about web page content. It is provided free of charge.

2. Data We Process

When you use Jaadu's AI features, the following data is transmitted to our backend server:

  • Page text content — when you click "Summarize Current Page" or use the Chat feature, the visible text of the current page is extracted and sent.
  • Selected text — when you right-click selected text and choose "Explain with Jaadu", that selected text is sent.
  • Questions you type — in the Chat and Explain tabs, the text you enter is sent to generate a response.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Text you submit is forwarded to the Google Gemini API solely to generate an AI response. It is processed in real time and not stored, logged, or retained by Jaadu's servers after the response is returned to you.

Google processes this data according to their own terms. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy and the Gemini API Terms of Service.

4. Data Stored Locally

Notes and pages you explicitly save via the "Save Note to Jaadu" or "Save Page to Jaadu" context menu options are stored locally in chrome.storage.local — on your device only. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us. You can delete it at any time from the Collection tab within the extension.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.
  • We do not collect browsing history or URLs.
  • We do not serve advertising of any kind.
  • We do not sell data to third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

Jaadu's backend is hosted on Render. Standard server infrastructure logs (IP address, request timestamps) may be retained by Render per their own policies. Jaadu does not access or use these logs.

AI processing is handled by Google Gemini. Please see Google's Gemini API Terms for details on how Google handles API inputs.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since Jaadu does not store personal data on its servers, there is no personal data to access, correct, export, or delete. Locally stored notes can be removed directly from within the extension (Collection tab → ✕ button).

If you have privacy-related questions, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Jaadu is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
